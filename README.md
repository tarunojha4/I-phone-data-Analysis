# iPhone Sales Analysis

Project Overview:
This project analyzes iPhones sold on Flipkart to gain insights into their pricing and ratings.

Key Highlights:
- Generated a list of the top 10 highest-rated iPhones.
- Investigated the relationship between iPhone pricing and user ratings.
- Assessed the impact of discounts to determine whether users tend to give higher ratings to lower-priced or higher-priced devices.
- Identified the most affordable and the most expensive iPhone models available.

Technologies Used:
- Python, Pandas, Plotly Express.

Key Findings:
The analysis revealed that lower-priced iPhones receive a higher volume of ratings, indicating that budget-friendly models are the 
top sellers in the Indian market.
